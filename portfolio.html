E-Commerce Platform
Project Overview

A fully responsive e-commerce platform with integrated payment gateways and inventory management for a retail business.

Client: TechRetail
Category: Web Development
Completion Date: June 2023
Project Details

This e-commerce platform was developed for TechRetail, a leading electronics retailer. The client needed a modern, responsive online store that could handle a large inventory of products and provide a seamless shopping experience for customers.

Key features of the platform include:

  • Responsive design for optimal viewing on all devices
  • Advanced product search and filtering options
  • Secure payment gateway integration
  • Real-time inventory management
  • Customer account management
  • Order tracking and history
  • Admin dashboard for managing products, orders, and customers

The platform was built using React.js for the frontend, Node.js for the backend, and MongoDB for the database. The result was a fast, secure, and user-friendly e-commerce website that helped the client increase their online sales by 45% within the first three months of launch.
